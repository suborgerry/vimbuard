What are the system requirements for Avast Small Office Protection?

Avast Small Office Protection requires your device to meet the following minimum system requirements:

  • PC: Windows 10, 8.1, 8, 7 (SP2) (32 or 64-bit), 1 GB RAM, 2 GB free hard disk space.
  • Mac: macOS 10.10 (Yosemite) or higher with 500 MB of hard disk space.
  • Android: Android 4.1 (Jelly Bean) or higher.
  • iPhone/iPad: iOS 10.0 or higher.

IMPORTANT: Avast Small Office Protection is not supported/is not compatible/cannot be installed/will not run on DOS or Microsoft Windows editions earlier than Windows 7, Microsoft Windows Server operating systems, or any others not specified as being supported.

Are there any license options for less than 10 devices?

The license includes up to 10 devices, meaning that you'll be able to use it for however many devices you have as long as it does not exceed 10 devices. At Avast, we know the modern workplace has changed. On average, employees use 2.5 devices to perform their jobs. They tend to use their personal laptops, smartphones, and tablets to stay connected from anywhere. To ensure protection for all devices, our Avast Small Office Protection provides a multi-device subscription to protect up to 10 devices, regardless of platform.

Is it necessary to uninstall my previous antivirus before I start using Avast Small Office Protection?

If you already have an Avast Business product without the Business Hub, you do not need to uninstall your current version. Avast antivirus setup automatically detects these versions and upgrades the existing installation to Avast Small Office Protection.
