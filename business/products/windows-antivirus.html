How do I install Avast Business Antivirus and Avast Business Antivirus Pro Plus?

We have published a helpful article that guides you through the installation process, step-by-step. If you experience any issues during installation, please submit a ticket to our business support team.

What level of protection will I receive with Avast Business Antivirus?

Avast Business Antivirus gives you essential protection against malware, ransomware, and spyware.

What level of protection will I receive with Avast Antivirus Pro Plus?

Avast Business Antivirus Pro Plus adds Identity Protection Components like Passwords, SecureLine VPN, Browser Cleanup, and Webcam Shield. You will receive advanced password protection, the ability to secure public networks and remove unnecessary browser data, and protection against webcam hacking.

How can I stay protected against malware and what are the best practices?

It is important to keep your Avast Business Antivirus up-to-date and run scans regularly. You can find all the useful information you’ll need in this article that suggests best practices when dealing with threats, along with more information on how to stay protected.

Where can I submit a false positive?

You can fill out a form on our page and submit the file/s or URL/s directly to our lab. After reviewing the false positive, a new virus definition will be released with a fix, and Avast will no longer detect the file/s or URL/s if they are in fact safe.
