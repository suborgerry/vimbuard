Have questions about Premium Support?

How is Premium Support different from Free Support?

Premium Support customers get 24/7 phone access to highly trained technical support engineers and receive priority queueing across all channels -- with a guaranteed response time of 1 hour or less. With Premium Support, you’ll also get remote installation, configuration, and deployment - so you can sit back and let us take care of the rest.

How can I track and see the status of my support request?

You can track all open support cases in your Avast Business Customer Portal, which can be accessed here: http://businessportal.avast.com/.

How many users can open technical support cases?

You can have as many users as you want with the ability to contact Premium Support and open tickets. Simply add the names of the users who should have access, along with the phone number they’ll use to call in, under Contacts in your account.

How quickly will you get back to me?

With Premium Support, you are guaranteed a 1 hour response time. We aim to quickly resolve your issue so you can get back to running your business.

What languages do you provide support in?

We currently only provide support in English.
