All-in-one cybersecurity solutions

Layered, cloud-based security designed to help MS(S)Ps keep their clients safe and protected, and elevate their business.

Meet CloudCare

Helping MSPs with their biggest challenges

Need to improve efficiency?
Many MSPs have limited resources and therefore are always on the lookout for a simple, scalable, easy-to-manage solution that would allow them to support the business growth of their customers and maximize their in-house resources.
Struggling to hire skilled staff?
Today, it is harder than ever to find qualified and skilled security professionals, as well as retain them. MSPs today require an all-in-one, comprehensive security platform that is easy to install and manage to reduce this dependency.
Looking to reduce your costs?
Running a successful business and maintaining profitability without compromising on service can be a struggle. MSPs require a reliable, all-in-one platform that enables them to increase their efficiency and reduce total TCO.

What is all-in-one cybersecurity?

In the past, managed service providers felt confident protecting customer devices and networks with just antivirus and perhaps a physical firewall appliance. Today, as everything moves online and to the cloud, security solutions must follow suit.

Traditional solutions expose your business customers to sophisticated security breaches. Legacy firewalls and data centers aren’t scalable and are costly to maintain. This leaves your business customers vulnerable to security gaps, including the risks associated with remote workers.

Yesterday’s network architecture is no longer sustainable in today’s environment. For total protection, your customers need all-in-one security - a combination of next-gen endpoint protection and cloud-based network security solutions that cover users anytime, anywhere.

Talk to an expert

Why should all-in-one security solutions be delivered from the cloud?

Cloud-based Traditional security
Security stays with the user. The business perimeter has dissolved. With cloud-based solutions, your customers’ users are protected anytime, anywhere.
Security is limited to the data center. Users outside the business perimeter are not protected from cyberattacks.
Simplicity. Phasing out appliances simplifies offerings and reduces costs. Our solutions are easy to use and maintain, requiring fewer resources.
Deploying and maintaining appliances from multiple security vendors is complex, requiring continuous patching, updates, and hardware upgrades.
Solutions are hosted in the cloud. No hardware-related fees, box installations, onsite visits, or refresh schedules. Manage everything from a single dashboard.
Solutions are hosted in a physical data center, costing companies valuable work space and requiring regular onsite visits.
Endless scalability. Add the latest features and endless users without breaking a sweat - or the bank.
Security doesn’t scale. Need to expand to a new office? This would require a new data center and significant upfront costs.
Comprehensive. Cloud security is able to scan every byte of incoming and outgoing traffic, including SSL and CDN.
Scanning is limited. Most traditional security appliances are unable to inspect encrypted (SSL) traffic.
Integrated. Consolidating point products into one platform, we make it easy to manage and protect customers’ devices, applications, data, and networks.
Managing multiple customers with disjointed, complex cybersecurity tools is challenging.

All-in-one solutions = multiple layers of security

As an MSP, you want to provide customers with best-in-class security, differentiate yourself from the competition, and increase profitability. Offering your customers a multi-layered security approach can provide the cornerstone for these objectives.

Data-level security

  • Cloud Backup - Avoid costly downtime with backup and recovery solutions that protect files and more.
  • Email Security - Secure email communications and block spam from mailboxes.

Endpoint-level security

Robust endpoint protection with:

  • Web Shield - Blocks dangerous websites before they open.
  • Smart Scan - Quickly checks for any performance or security issues.
  • CyberCapture - Sends suspicious files to the Threat Lab for analysis.
  • Behavior Shield - Stops dangerous programs and applications on your device.

Application-level security

  • Patch Management - Identifies critical vulnerabilities and quickly deploys updates to endpoints

Network-level security

Secure gateways to protect unauthorized access and block threats traveling through encrypted traffic, anywhere, anytime.
